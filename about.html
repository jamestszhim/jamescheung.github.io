My Personality

The MBTI test

Personally, I quite like to describe my personality using the MBTI since it describe me quite well. MBTI is a theory constructed by Katharine Cook Briggs and her daughter Isabel Briggs Myers to describe the order of each person’s preference and choice in 4 areas. A person will be described using a 4-letter acronym, which comes from:

  • Introversion (I) or Extraversion (E)
  • Intuition (N) or Sensing (S)
  • Thinking (T) or Feeling (F)
  • Judging (J) or Perceiving (P)

The ESFJ

So, I am of the type ESFJ, that means I am Extraversion, Sensing, Feeling and Judging. In general, ESFJ people, including me, are extraordinarily caring, social and popular people, always eager to help.

Through understanding my personality I learnt to adjust myself and collaborate with others. In some group projects, I invited my teammates taking the test and we turned out working really well.
